乐学班
Happy learning Class (K1 - K2)

K1 - Further improve the ability in listening and speaking, and recognizing; Understand the structure of characters, and learn to write characters.
K1-提高听、说、认的能力，了解汉字的结构，学习写字。

K2 - Equip children with some ability to read, recognize and comprehend Chinese characters before they enter primary school; Get them prepared for Primary 1.
K2-让孩子在进入小学前，有一定的阅读、写字、理解能力，为小一做准备。

Nursery Rhymes / Pattern Drills /Strokes of Characters/ Recognizing and Writing Characters / Stories Telling
背儿歌、认字、句型练习、数笔画、写字、讲故事

Recognizing and Spelling Characters/ Basic Components and Radicals of Characters / Hanyu Pinyin
认偏旁部首、听写、汉语拼音、讲故事

阅读理解班
Comprehension & Composition Class (P1- P6)

Follow the latest syllabus issued by the Ministry of Education, consolidate Mandarin foundation, improve passage analysis and answering skills.
紧跟教育部最新教学纲要，巩固华文基础，重点提升阅读理解能力、写、作、听力、会话和做题技巧。

Listening Comprehension/ Usage of Words in Textbook/Analysis of Essays (P3-P6)/Training on Examination type Questions
阅读理解、课文字词运用、文章分析、写作技巧、会话能力、听力训练、考试题型训练

说读趣味班
Reading & Speaking Class (P1- P6)

Focus on listening, speaking, reading and comprehension, revolve around the text and oral test requirements to provide relaxing and entertaining exercise.
以听、说、读和理解为重点，围绕课本内容和口试要求进行较为轻松、有趣的训练。

Listening Comprehension Recognizing Characters / Comprehension of Text / Reading aloud / Picture Discussion / Oral Conversation
听力、认字、课文阅读与理解、朗读、看图说话、会话

阅读写作班
Composition & Oral Class (P2-P6)

Focus on enhancement of composition skills and oral capability as well as comprehensive language application capability and enrichment of vocabulary.
重点加强写作能力训练，阅读理解、听力以及会话技巧，丰富词汇量。

Composition Skills / Oral Skills / Analysis of Essays(P3-P6)/Comprehension
写作训练和技巧、口语表达、听力训练、文章分析（小三-小六）、阅读理解训练

火箭全能班
All in One Class (P3 - P6)

Follow the latest syllabus issued by the Ministry of Education, improve passage analysis, oral and listening skill, power up writing skill, increase vocabulary and article appreciation.
紧跟教育部最新教学纲要，重点提升阅读理解能力、写作、听力、会话和做题技巧，增加识字量和美文欣赏。

Composition Skills / Oral Skills / Listening Skills/Analysis of Essays / Comprehension/ Listening Comprehension/ Higher Chinese material added
写作训练和技巧、口语表达、听力训练、文章分析、阅读理解、会话能力，增加高级华文考试题型（作文、理解问答）

中学进修班
Enrichment Class(Sec1 - Sec4)

Closely follow the latest syllabus issued by the Ministry of Education, Focus on enhancement of comprehend and oral capability ,improve composition skills.
紧跟教育部最新教学纲要，重点加强阅读理解和写作能力的训练，会话、听力以及答题技巧训练。

Usage of Words in Textbook / Analysis of Essays/ Extension of Vocabulary /Comprehensive Comprehension / Oral Conversation /Composition Skills
课文字词运用、文章分析、词汇扩充、阅读理解、会话、听力、大小作文训练。
